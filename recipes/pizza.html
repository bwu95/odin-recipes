Pizza Recipe

A scrumptious pizza recipe

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy but has become a global favorite. To make a classic pizza, you'll need to start by making or purchasing pizza dough, which can be made from scratch using flour, yeast, salt, and water. Then, you can spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough and sprinkle on shredded mozzarella cheese. After that, you can add toppings of your choice, such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, or olives. Finally, you'll bake the pizza in a preheated oven at around 425°F (218°C) for approximately 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Once it's done, you can slice the pizza and serve it hot and fresh for a delicious meal that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • pepperoni
  • mushrooms
  • onions or olives

Cooking Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Roll out your pizza dough onto a floured surface until it reaches the desired size and thickness.
  3. Transfer the pizza dough to pizza stone or a baking sheet.
  4. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.
  6. Add your desired toppings over the cheese
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  9. Enjoy the delicious homemade pizza! Note: Please be careful, the pizza will be very hot!
